Brown Sugar Milk Tea

Description

Wanna know how to make a brown sugar milk tea that tastes just the way it does at Surreal Creamery? Well you're in luck! This is definitely not their recipe that I got from working there, I'm just a master chef so I can craft any recipe in the world! Get ready because this is a super easy recipe and tastes great muahaha!!

Ingredients

Medium

  • 200ml Earl Gray Black Tea
  • .75 tbsp of Sugar
  • 9 tbsp of powdered milk
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ice

Large

  • 300ml Earl Gray Black Tea
  • 1 tbsp of Sugar
  • 10 tbsp of powdered milk
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ice

Steps

Whatever size you decide to make, the steps are the same!

  1. Find a shaker cup, this is where you are going to be making the milk tea
  2. Pour your required amount of sugar into shaker cup
  3. Pour your required amount of powdered into shaker cup
  4. Pour your required amount of hot black tea into shaker cup
  5. Mix all of your powders/sugars into your tea well until the powder gets fully disolved into the tea and takes on a light brown color
  6. Fill the rest of your shaker cup with ice and shake shake shake!! (make sure the tea becomes cold, if it hasn't you might need to throw some more ice into your cup or find a bigger shaker cup and put more ice)
  7. Take your drinking cup/glass and pour a generous amount of brown sugar in, dazzling the inside and rim of your glass to perfection

    8. Voala!! Your brown sugar Milk tea is finished. If you would like to add any boba or tapioca you can do so! However make sure to do this before step 7.
