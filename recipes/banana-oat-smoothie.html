Delicious Banana Oat Smoothie Recipe

Description

Now the picture I included isn't actually what my smoothie looks like as I was too lazy to take a picture of my smoothie. Buttttt, it looks pretty damn similar if not identical. Anyways.... If you want to make a super duper gas banana oat smoothie look no further because I've got you covered. This soothie will make you feel refreshed and full. And it uses natural ingredients so you can feel good and healthy after!

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp Vanila Extract
  • 1 Frozen Banana (peeled)
  • 1 Spoonfull of Peanut butter
  • .75 cups of milk
  • 1 tsp Maple Syrup
  • 3/4 cup of dry oats
  • 1 tbsp of flax seeds
  • Dash of cinammon

Steps

  1. Find a blender!
  2. Pour milk into blender
  3. Put the rest of the listed ingredients into the blender
  4. Blend until there are no chunks and you have smooth consistency as seen in the picture

Vioala!! You're smoothie is done and should be super duper delicious now
